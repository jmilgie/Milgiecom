# Emberhollow Material Pack

This pack tracks the environment and prop surfaces we want the game to ship with as local generated textures.

## Core Ground + Wall Surfaces

- `town-floor`: Bellroot paths and outdoor hub paving
- `town-wall`: Bellroot and Starlight Row timber-and-plaster walls
- `moss-floor`: first cave layer ground
- `moss-wall`: first cave layer walls and stone props
- `reservoir-floor`: water-adjacent chamber floors
- `reservoir-wall`: water-adjacent chamber walls
- `echo-floor`: resonant crystal cave floors
- `echo-wall`: resonant crystal cave walls
- `forge-floor`: furnace and ember forge floors
- `forge-wall`: furnace and ember forge walls
- `archive-floor`: Moonlit Archive floors
- `archive-wall`: Moonlit Archive walls
- `boss-floor`: Choir King arena floor
- `boss-wall`: Choir King arena walls

## Shared Prop Surfaces

- `roof-shingles`: town roofs and shelter tops
- `tree-bark`: trunks, poles, and rough vertical timber
- `woven-trim`: cloth props, banners, robes, and hazard fabric fallback
- `stone-blocks`: generic carved stone for benches, tables, pedestals, and fountain bases
- `forge-metal`: anvils, rails, braces, and forge-side metal props
- `archive-paper`: maps, notes, books, and archive paper props

## FX + Specialty Surfaces

- `water-surface`: reflective pools and reservoir water
- `crystal-facets`: crystal props, anchors, and luminous shards
- `rune-hazard`: glowing trap plates, boss runes, and magical hazard accents

## Batch Goal

Complete the list above as one coherent texture pass so each theme has its own floor/wall identity and the shared prop materials stop relying on placeholder procedural textures.
