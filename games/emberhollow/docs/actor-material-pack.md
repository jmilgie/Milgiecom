# Emberhollow Actor Material Pack

This pack covers the reusable textures for NPCs, enemies, and the boss.

## Human + NPC Surfaces

- `skin-aged`: elder faces and hands
- `skin-young`: younger NPC faces and hands
- `hair-strands`: reusable hair texture for silver and dark hair
- `cloth-frontier`: everyday robes, shawls, and layered frontier clothes
- `cloth-brocade`: bard and ceremonial trims
- `cloth-explorer`: cartographer coats, packs, and field layers
- `cloth-glass`: glassweaver drapes and refined workshop garments
- `leather-worn`: belts, boots, satchels, gloves
- `apron-soot`: smith apron and forge worker layers

## Enemy + Boss Surfaces

- `crawler-carapace`: crawler shell plates
- `crawler-underhide`: crawler flesh/underbody
- `warden-armor`: warden plate and helm surfaces
- `wisp-energy`: translucent spectral shell pattern
- `choir-obsidian`: boss body plates and dark ceremonial shell
- `choir-gilded`: boss crown and sacred metallic trim

## Detail Overlays

- `face-elder`: elder facial decal
- `face-smith`: smith facial decal
- `face-bard`: bard facial decal
- `face-cartographer`: cartographer facial decal
- `face-glassweaver`: glassweaver facial decal
- `wisp-runes`: floating spectral glyph overlay
- `warden-runes`: visor and chest sigil overlay
- `crawler-eyes`: close-range glowing eye band
- `choir-runes`: boss halo and ceremonial rune overlay
- `choir-heart-mask`: boss heart fissure overlay

## Boss-Only Surfaces

- `choir-veil`: ceremonial hanging fabric for banners and accents
- `choir-heart-surface`: close-up heart shell texture for the Choir King core

## Batch Goal

Use this pack to support significantly more detailed NPC, monster, and boss models with readable textured surfaces instead of plain colored primitives.
