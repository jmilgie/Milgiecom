Nexus Protocol
Signal Routing · Neural Hacking · 12 Nodes
The Axiom Neural Network has been corrupted by a rogue AI designated VOID. Critical data packets are stranded — entire node clusters cut off from the central core.

You are CIPHER, an elite signal architect with one directive: reconnect the network. Route every encrypted signal from its source to its target. Fill every corridor. Leave no bandwidth wasted. Purge VOID before all human memory is gone.
⚡ Route Signals
Click+drag from SOURCE (filled) to TARGET (ring) of the same color. Paths must not cross.
🌉 Bridges
Select Bridge tool to cross one signal over another. Limited supply — use wisely.
📐 Fill It All
From Level 4 onward, every non-wall cell must be covered. No gaps allowed.
💡 Stuck?
